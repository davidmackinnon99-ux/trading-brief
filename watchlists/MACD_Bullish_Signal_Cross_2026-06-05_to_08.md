## Scan definition
Stocks where the MACD line crossed ABOVE the signal line (12/26 EMA relationship) -
a bullish crossover marking emerging momentum or trend reversal. 2-day lookback so a
single missed run doesn't drop a fresh cross. Same condition as the validated LORP
**MACD0** gate (confirmed 2026-06-09 as the one LORP entry factor separating winners
from losers).

**Filters applied on transcription:** Class A only - dropped secondary share classes: NASDAQ:NWS.

> NOTE 1: the export's `date` column stamps the SCAN RUN date (all rows 2026-06-08), NOT
> the per-ticker cross day - so a 2-day scan widens the net but does not tell you which
> of the two days each name crossed. To know the cross day you'd compare against the
> prior day's list (which is why keeping dated snapshots in this folder is useful).
> NOTE 2: blank-sector rows are ETFs, not single stocks: AMEX:KBE, NYSE:SHV, NASDAQ:TBIL.
> SHV / TBIL are cash-like Treasury ETFs - a MACD cross on them is mechanically
> meaningless (near-flat), treat as noise.
