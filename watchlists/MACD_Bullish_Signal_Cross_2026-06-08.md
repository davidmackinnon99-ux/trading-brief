# MACD Bullish Signal Line Cross - Scan Watchlist

**Source:** SBT scan export | **Snapshot date:** 2026-06-08 | **Members:** 19

## Scan definition
Identifies stocks where the MACD line has crossed ABOVE the signal line - a classic
bullish crossover marking the start of positive momentum or a trend reversal. MACD
measures the relationship between the 12- and 26-period EMAs; the faster MACD line
crossing above the slower signal line suggests bullish momentum building. Used to:
spot bear->bull reversals, find momentum stocks starting a new upward leg, find
entries after consolidation/correction, and combine with price action or volume for
confirmation. Most effective when confirmed by other signals.

> This SBT MACD-cross definition (MACD line vs signal) is the same condition as the
> validated LORP **MACD0** gate (MACD vs Signal) - confirmed 2026-06-09 as the one
> LORP entry factor that separated winners from losers (0/17 winners crossed down).
